## The Philosophy of the Roadmap

Most calisthenics content online is built for athletes who are 1.70m and 65kg. When you are operating with long levers (1.88m) and heavy mass (95kg), the physics of bodyweight mastery change entirely. 

This roadmap is designed to cut through the noise. It is the exact sequence I follow and recommend for building a chassis capable of elite strength. We aren't just doing endless push-ups; we are preparing the joints, tendons, and muscles for high-intensity loads.
